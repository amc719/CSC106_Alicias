Final Project

About the program:

For my final project, I created a "fortune teller" type game. Answer the questions as honestly as possible for the most accurate prediction. The game may even predict your horoscope, or give you that piece of advice you didn't know you needed!
You can play by clicking the start button. On each page, read the question and click on the answer that you agree with the most. Continue to answer questions until your fortune is revealed! Mwuahahaha!
